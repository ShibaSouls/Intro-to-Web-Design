Operating in Ohio for over 5 years, Riggi Rescue is determined to rescue, offer care, and adopt out as many cats as we can.

We also combine our mission of saving cats with community assistance by trapping, neutering and releasing ("TNR") local feral colonies. Our pawsome and skilled trappers have been successful in several local TNR missions, saving hundreds of lives in the process.
